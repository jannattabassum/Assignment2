Flower Details

Roses are known for their beautiful appearance and fragrant smell.

Tulips come in a variety of vibrant colors and are often associated with spring.

Lilies are elegant and have a pleasant fragrance.

Interesting Facts

  • Roses have thorns to protect themselves from predators.
  • Tulips originated in the Ottoman Empire.
  • Lilies are often associated with purity and rebirth.
  • Daisies symbolize innocence and simplicity.
  • Sunflowers track the sun's movement across the sky.
